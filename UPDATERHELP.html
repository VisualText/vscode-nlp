Solutions to NLP Engine Updating Timeout

If your NLP Engine is timing out, then try these suggestions:

  • Check internet connection
    • If your internet connection is not working, the NLP++ Language Extension updater will timeout.
    • Your internet connection is slow or intermittent so the updater times out
  • NLP++ Language Extension is Updating
    • In very rare cases, the NLP++ Language Extension latest version is corrupted
    • Or the extension is being updated and you can try updating in a minute or so
